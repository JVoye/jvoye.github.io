    • Damien Breier

    Vice President

    Mr. Breier has over 20 years of experience in the design and construction of material handling systems. As Vice President, Mr. Breier's responsibilities include conceptual design, generation of mathematical models to facilitate analyses of complex systems, contractor's design review, project management, and supervision of implementation monitoring and systems testing.


    His work focuses primarily on large scale international hub projects for passenger and cargo terminals. With previous full time on-site assignments as a project manager at four major international airports, Mr. Breier had gained valuable experience that allows him to provide innovative and elegant designs, resolve issues ranging from planning approvals to operational work conditions and handle contractual issues between owners and contractors.


    He has designed and implemented systems on 6 continents and in more than 30 countries providing a world class foundation of knowledge and experience that he brings to each project.
